PRIVACY POLICY

inDest understands that privacy is important to our customers and that you care about how your personal data is used and shared online.

At inDest we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our customers and will only collect and use personal data in ways that are described in this policy and in line with your rights under the law.

Please read the following Privacy Policy carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your personal data and how we will treat it.

Your acceptance of our Privacy Policy is deemed to occur upon your first use of our site. If you do not accept and agree with this Privacy Policy, you must stop using our site immediately.

Please note that this Privacy Policy only applies to the use of our site, which may contain links to other sites. We do not have control over how your data is collected, stored, or used by other sites and therefore advise you to check their privacy policies before providing any data to them.

We use information held about you in the following ways:

For our b2b-clients:

      2. To ensure that content from our site is presented in the most effective manner for you and for your computer.
      3. To provide you with information, products, or services that you request from us or which we feel may interest you, where you have consented to be contacted for such purposes.
      4. To carry out our obligations arising from any contracts entered between you and us.
      5. To allow you to participate in interactive features of our service when you choose to do so.
      6. To notify you about changes to our service.
      7. To contact you if we believe you have a legitimate interest in receiving the information. For more information about our legitimate interest policy, which is detailed below.
      8. We may also use your data, or permit selected third parties to use your data, to provide you with information about products or services which may be of interest to you and we or they may contact you about these by email.
      9. If you are an existing customer, we will only contact you by electronic means (e-mail or SMS) with information about products and services like those which were the subject of a previous sale to you.
      10. If you are a new customer, and where we permit selected third parties to use your data, we (or they) will only contact you by electronic means if you have consented to this.

RETENTION POLICY

Other than data we need to retain for legal purposes such as transaction history, we will only keep your data on file for a maximum of three months. This includes any emails sent to info@indest.travel.

WHERE WE STORE YOUR PERSONAL DATA

The data that we collect from you may be transferred to and stored at, a destination outside the European Economic Area ("EEA"). It may also be processed by staff operating outside the EEA, who work for us or for one of our suppliers. Such staff may be engaged in, among other things, the fulfillment of client orders, the processing of client payment details, and the provision of support services. By submitting your personal data, you agree to all transfer, storing, or processing of your personal data. inDestination AB will take all steps reasonably necessary to ensure that your data is treated securely and in accordance with this privacy policy.

All information you provide to us is stored on our secure servers. Any payment transactions between clients and inDestination AB will be encrypted (using SSL technology). Where we have given you (or where you have chosen) a password that enables you to access certain parts of our site, you are responsible for keeping this password confidential. We ask you not to share a password with anyone.

Unfortunately, the transmission of information via the internet is not completely secure. Although we will do our best to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee the security of your data transmitted to our site; any transmission is at your own risk. Once we have received your information, we will use strict procedures and security features to try to prevent unauthorized access.

This to protect you, but we can exclude your fully IP-address by contacting info@idest.travel.

For the same reason, we may obtain information about your general internet usage by using a cookie file that is stored on the hard drive of your computer. Cookies contain information that is transferred to your computer's hard drive. They help us to improve our site and to deliver a better and more personalized service. They enable us:

  • To estimate our audience size and usage pattern;
  • To store information about your preferences, and so allow us to customize our site according to your individual interest;

For further information, you can read our full cookie policy.

COOKIE POLICY

When you visit our site, we use cookies to provide you with the best experience possible. There are 4 types of cookies, which are explained below.

(1) Strictly Necessary Cookies – These cookies allow you to navigate the site and use the site’s features.

(2) Performance Cookies – this allows us to understand the number of users our site receives, how they use it, and where we can make improvements for the benefit of our customers. We may also use this anonymized information to create internal reports and to help us improve the website.

(3) Customer preference cookies – when you are browsing on our sites, these cookies will remember your preferences, so we can make your experience as continuous and seamless as possible.

(4) Targeting or advertising cookies – These cookies help deliver targeted ads with content that we would expect you to be interested in, based on your shopping behaviors. Importantly, they also limit the number of times that you will see an ad from this site.

By continuing to use this site, you are implying consent to the placement of the cookies listed and for the purposes also described above. By using our site, you agree to the placement of these sorts of cookies on your device and accessing them when you visit the site in the future.

If you do want to delete or prevent any cookies that are already on your computer, you can do so within your chosen browser, and visit the settings. Please note that by deleting or disabling future cookies, your on-site experience may be affected, and you might not be able to take advantage of some of the certain functions of our site.
